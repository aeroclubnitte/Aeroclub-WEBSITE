Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 05-07-2025

Shipping is not applicable as AeroClub Nitte is a student club and not a commercial business.

We do not deliver physical products. All communication, certificates, and updates will be shared digitally via registered emails or college-approved platforms.

For delivery-related concerns, please contact us at aeroclub.nmamit@nitte.edu.in.

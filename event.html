RC Workshop

Aeroclub Nitte organized an RC Aeromodelling Workshop at NMAM Institute of Technology on October 5th and 6th, 2024, from 9:00 am to 5:30 pm. The event saw 149 participants from various branches, guided by 37 core team volunteers across 24 teams. Four glue stations and one simulation setup enhanced the learning experience, with each participant enjoying 5 minutes of simulation time. A dedicated team of 33 volunteers assisted on both days.

Quadcopter Workshop

The Aeroclub Nitte conducted a Quadcopter Workshop at NMAMIT on 16-17 November 2024, with 57 participants divided into 10 teams and supported by 24-25 volunteers and a social media team. The workshop included presentations on quadcopter basics, electronics, and mission planning, followed by hands-on assembly and simulations. Teams completed their quadcopters and conducted successful flight tests despite minor rain and technical issues. Positive feedback highlighted active participation, efficient volunteer support, and an engaging learning experience. The workshop was led by Shainil S Shetty and Chinmay Kulkarni under Dr. Gururaj Upadhyaya’s guidance.

Outreach Tech-Talk

The AeroClub of NMAM Institute of Technology, in collaboration with the STACS Team of NRAM Polytechnic College, conducted an outreach talk on UAVs and RC planes on November 30th at Shambhavi Auditorium. Key topics, including UAV basics, quadcopter dynamics, RC planes, and avionics, were presented by AeroClub members. The session featured live demonstrations of RC planes and a quadcopter, offering attendees practical insights. The event successfully inspired interest in aeronautics among students and faculty.

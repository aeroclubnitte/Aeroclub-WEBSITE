CLASS OF 2025

Wright Flight

Wright Flight 2024, held on October 17-18 at NIT Karnataka as part of ENGINEER’24, featured five teams from Aeroclub Nitte. Teams competed in obstacle and payload rounds, showcasing exceptional skill and resilience. Team Ghatotkach won 1st place with flawless payload dropping, while Team Gravity Defiers secured 2nd place. All teams demonstrated technical expertise and teamwork, making the event a great learning experience.

SAE DDC

The Aero Club Nitte team proudly clinched 1st place in the overall category at the prestigious SAE DDC 2024, outperforming 54 formidable teams! Held from August 29th to 31st at SRM University, Chennai, this renowned competition, organized by SAE India Southern Section, tested our skills across various aeronautics and engineering disciplines. We set a remarkable record in the flying round, successfully assembling an aircraft within a constrained timeframe and showcasing its superior performance with a payload.


Aerodash- VIT,Vellore

Remarkable event is the 1st place victory at the VIT Aero Dash 2024, held on August 8th and 9th in Vellore, and organized by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME). Competing against some of the best teams, our success was driven by innovative designs, technical precision, and exceptional flight performance. The competition required teams to optimize aircraft for maximum payloads while adhering to strict guidelines on launch accuracy and timing. The outstanding efforts in both the technical presentation and flight rounds showcased their dedication to innovation and engineering excellence. This achievement is ongoing commitment to advancing aviation technology through collaboration and precision.


SAE ADDC

AeroClub Nitte is here with one more announcement about winning 1st place at the SAE Drone Development Competition 2024, held at KCG Tech Engineering College, Chennai, on July 19th and 20th. Competing against 47 teams, we excelled across three rigorous rounds: technical inspection, flight performance, and presentation.


Boeing National Aeromodelling Competition Finals, RVCE Bengaluru

Aeroclub has achieved a historic feat, claiming the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd places in the finals of the prestigious Boeing National Aeromodelling Competition Finals held at RVCE, Bengaluru. This extraordinary accomplishment underscores the unwavering dedication, technical prowess, and competitive spirit of our team members.

Boeing IIT National Aeromodelling Competition-Kharagpur

This outstanding feat not only strengthens our standing but also propels three of our teams to qualify for the finals. SAE drone design Competition

Collaboratively with a committed team of 10 individuals across interdisciplinary courses and with the consistent support of the team, we have secured rank 1, claiming the top position in a prestigious SAE drone design competition. Our outstanding aircraft stood out as the exclusive contender capable of carrying a payload exceeding 2 kg. Notably, We take immense pride in being the sole female pilot to achieve this remarkable feat in the competition..

CLASS OF 2022

SAE Aerodesign Challenge

AIR 2

Enduring 10 months of unwavering commitment during the challenges posed by the COVID times, our team of 7 students from diverse interdisciplinary branches, supported by dedicated mentors, participated in the SAE Aerodesign Challenge post-pandemic.

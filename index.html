About Us

Aero Club Nitte aims to become a leading force in drone technology and innovation by fostering technical excellence, building a vibrant aviation community, and expanding its global influence. Our approach includes hosting RC aeromodelling workshops, engaging in outreach programs, and pursuing collaborative opportunities. We have a proven track record of success, having won numerous competitions and actively participating in prestigious events at IITs, where we have consistently achieved top honors

VISION

To become a leading force in drone technology and innovation, Aero Club, Nitte envisions fostering technical excellence, building a vibrant aviation community, and expanding its global influence through RC aeromodelling workshops, outreach programs, and collaboration

MISSION

Our mission is to share knowledge and expertise while nurturing a vibrant and influential aviation community, all underpinned by our steadfast commitment to excellence as we continually inspire and propel our members to achieve remarkable success in the aviation realm

What We Do

AeroClub is where innovation meets aviation. From RC planes to drones, we design, build, and fly, pushing the limits of aerospace technology.

RC Aeromodelling Workshop

A multi-rotor workshop is a hands-on event where participants learn to build and fly their own drones, gaining practical skills in drone assembly and flight operation. It's an ideal introduction to the world of drone technology and offers a fun and educational experience.

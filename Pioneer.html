Gururaj Upadhyaya

Dr. Gururaj Upadhyaya has been the backbone of our club since its inception, playing a pivotal role in its success and development. As our technical coordinator, he has provided consistent guidance in every aspect, offering invaluable support and sharing his vast expertise. His mentorship has been instrumental in shaping the club’s numerous achievements, fostering growth and innovation within the team. PIONEER



Ananth Erasappa

Ananth Erasappa: The Heart of Aeroclub and Visionary of Vayurvya As president of our Aeroclub, Ananth Erasappa has been more than a leader; he’s been a guide and mentor, especially in realizing our dream of Vayurvya. His dedication and expertise have empowered us and shaped our journey in aeronautics. Ananth’s leadership is the cornerstone of our club’s identity. Through highs and lows, he has been a constant presence, supporting us through challenges and celebrating victories. His unwavering commitment inspires us to strive for excellence while staying grounded in our passion. Thanks to his guidance, Aeroclub has achieved new heights, competing at local and national levels. Ananth and his team have led us to transform our dreams into reality, shaping a journey that has been both inspiring and transformative. We are deeply grateful for their leadership and commitment.

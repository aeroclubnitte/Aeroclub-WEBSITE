SURVELLIANCE DRONE

The autonomous drone, weighing approximately 2.5 kg, is configured in an H shape for optimal stability during perimeter surveillance. It employs a Pixhawk flight controller for precise autonomous operations, with Mission Planner serving as the ground control station (GCS). The onboard Raspberry Pi enhances computational capabilities, and the drone is versatile enough for payload dropping.

Surveillance Drone

AUTONOMOUS PLANE

An autonomous fixed wing UAV for payload dropping and medical kit is an unmanned aerial vehicle that has a fixed wing design and can fly without human intervention to deliver medical supplies or equipment to a desired location. Autonomous fixed wing UAVs can be useful for emergency situations, rural areas, or remote locations where conventional delivery methods are not feasible or safe.
